OUR SERVICES

We Provide

CVOR

COMMERCIAL VEHICLE OPERATORS REGISTRATION.
CVOR stands for Commercial Vehicle Operator Registration, and it is a program that ensures that all commercial vehicles and their operators are operating safely and within the law.

Read More
IFTA

INTERNATIONAL FUEL TAX AGREEMENT.
IFTA stands for the International Fuel Tax Agreement, and it is an agreement between the lower 48 United States and the 10 Canadian provinces that simplifies the reporting of fuel use taxes by commercial carriers operating in multiple jurisdictions.

Read More
IRP

INTERNATIONAL REGISTRATION PLAN
IRP stands for the International Registration Plan, and it is an agreement between the lower 48 United States, the District of Columbia, and Canadian provinces that allows commercial vehicles to be registered and licensed to operate in multiple jurisdictions using a single registration plate.

Read More
UCR

UNIFIED CARRIER REGISTRATION
UCR (Unified Carrier Registration) is a program that was established under the Unified Carrier Registration Act of 2005. It is a federal program that requires all carriers operating in interstate commerce to register with a participating state and pay an annual fee.

Read More
NEW MEXICO WEIGHT DISTANCE TAX PERMIT

The New Mexico Weight Distance Tax Permit is a tax permit that commercial vehicle owners must obtain to operate within the state. The permit is required for any vehicle with a gross weight of 26,001 pounds or more

Read More
NEW YORK HUT (HIGHWAY TAX)

Welcome to the New York Hut (Highway Use Tax) program, a system that helps fund the construction, maintenance, and repair of highways, bridges, and other transportation infrastructure throughout New York State.

Read More
KENTUCKY REGISTRATION

Kentucky requires all vehicles, including commercial vehicles, to be registered with the state. Vehicle registration in Kentucky is handled by the Division of Motor Vehicle Licensing (DMVL) and must be renewed annually.

Read More
OREGON FILE NUMBER

Welcome to the Oregon File Number program, a system that provides a unique identifier for businesses operating in the state of Oregon. The program is administered by the Oregon Secretary of State

Read More
OPERATING AUTHORITY (MC/FF/MX NUMBER)

Welcome to the Operating Authority (MC/FF/MX Number) program, a system that regulates and authorizes the operation of commercial vehicles in the United States. The program applies to all motor carriers that transport goods or passengers across state lines and is administered by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA).

Read More
USDOT NUMBER

A USDOT number is a unique identifier assigned by the United States Department of Transportation (USDOT) to commercial motor vehicles that transport passengers or cargo in interstate commerce. In Ontario, carriers that operate commercial motor vehicles in interstate commerce are required to obtain a USDOT number from the USDOT.

Read More
QUEBEC

QUEBEC REGISTRATION
Welcome to the Quebec Registration program, a system that regulates and authorizes the operation of commercial vehicles in the province of Quebec. The program applies to all motor carriers that transport goods or passengers within the province and is administered by the Quebec Ministry of Transport.
